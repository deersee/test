About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face waves of monstrous creatures in a post-apocalyptic world. Your goal is simple: survive as long as possible while defeating as many monsters as you can.

With intuitive controls and progressively challenging gameplay, Monster Survivors offers an engaging experience for both casual and hardcore gamers.

This game is developed using Unity engine and runs directly in your browser with no downloads required.
